The Challenge

The legacy .NET compliance platform was disorganized and hard to manage. As the AI team formed, we recognized the opportunity to ease the burden on the human compliance team by automating manual tasks and streamlining workflows..

The Solution

With guidance from stakeholders bringing 22+ years of compliance experience, we conducted thorough research to uncover key challenges within existing human-led compliance processes, alongside domain-focused product managers. Using LLM models and agent-based workflows, we designed a platform that reduced manual effort and enabled scalable, automated compliance operations.
